A Chat With Pia Celestino

Here at for{ada}, every week we spotlight a woman or girl in tech who inspires us. This week we were lucky enough to meet and learn from Pia Celestino. Pia is a very successful businesswoman who is flourishing in the tech industry. Not only is she the co-founder of the EyeTalker, she is the Creative Director and founder of the company Crea7ive. In 2012, she co-founded the EyeTalker. During this project she developed a pair of glasses with a camera which captures printed text and delivers it as audio to the visually impaired. In 2013 she then went on to give her first TED talk on these glasses. Her company Crea7ive is a branding and digital marketing agency based in Miami, FL. Under Pia's leadership, Crea7ive has been featured on Inc. Magazine, Forbes, received numerous awards and has worked with popular brands such as Wendy's, Disney, Kraft, Armani Casa and Dreamworks (Rango the movie).



Recently, Pia visited a Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program Class and spoke to its girls about the importance of managing expectations in a very competitive environment and achieving success and happiness from within while excelling at work. She was very down to earth and encouraged the girls to be successful.

Our Mission:

Our purpose is simple: to close the gender gap in the field of technology. By providing girls with a safe space to learn and the resources they need to pursue tech careers, we hope to fulfill our mission and in turn fill the industry with diversity.
