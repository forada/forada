Contribute

Call for submissions! Share your TECH-TASTIC thoughts with other girls just like you. We would love to hear from you but before you submit, here are a few guidlines and suggestions (be sure to check back as we will keep adding to this list)

Content

You can submit articles of any sort as long as they fall under one of the six categories we have on our site: Spotlight, Nonprofit Organizations, Tech Companies, Awareness, and Discover. Interviews are great too but you must first ask for our permission. UNDER NO CIRCUMSTANCE will you use our name to gain an interview without our permission!!

Original Work Only

All work must be original and unpublished. Meaning it can not be found anywhere before you submit it to us.

  • Any form of Social Media
  • Other Publications
  • Your own personal blog or website

Where to submit

Send your work to us by email @ for4ada@gmail.com

Remember to include:
  • Name
  • Age (p.s. we only accept work from 10 year olds to 19 year olds)
  • Intro to your piece (5-9 sentences explaining your piece)
  • How you came across our website

BE YOU!! Remember to write content that is pleasing to you! We don't want to read an article that you thought we would like. The whole point of collaborating is so that we can showcase multiple personalities through our Newsletter.
